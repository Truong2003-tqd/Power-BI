### Module 1: Excel Foundation 
**Materials:** Excel for Data Analytics - Full Course for Beginners -  by Luke Barousse - 11 hours - Free

**Link:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pCJ15nGFgVg&list=PL_CkpxkuPiT-RJ7zBfHVWwgltEWIVwrwb

**Description:** 
Roadmap from setup through formulas, visualization, advanced analytics, and two dashboard projects.

**Learning Outcomes:**
- Configure Excel for analytics work and navigate worksheets, workbooks, and the ribbon confidently.
- Build formulas with logical, math, statistical, lookup, text, and date/time functions to solve real business problems.
- Design clear visualizations with charts, sparklines, and dashboard elements tailored to stakeholder stories.
- Apply tables, formatting, collaboration tools, PivotTables, and add-ins to analyze and summarize large datasets efficiently.
- Use Power Query, Power Pivot, and DAX to shape data models, automate refreshes, and deliver end-to-end analytics projects.

### Module 2: Financial Budgeting with Excel

**Materials:** A Comprehensive Excel Masterclass -  by Illinois Tech - 2 months (10h/week) - Free

**Link:** https://www.coursera.org/learn/illinois-tech-a-comprehensive-excel-masterclass-unleashing-business-potential

**Description:** 
Advanced Excel track covering complex functions, financial modeling, capital budgeting, and interactive dashboard design for business decisions.

**Learning Outcomes:**

- Utilize complex Excel functions and techniques to extract and manipulate data.
- Evaluate the financial viability of loans using Excel's financial functions.
- Apply the principles of Capital Budgeting for project selection and investment decisions.
- Create advanced Pivot Tables and Pivot Charts for data analysis and visualization.
- Develop interactive dashboards with Form Controls to enhance user experience.
- Showcase key performance indicators (KPIs) to support data-driven decision-making in business intelligence.
